Animecon is an anime based meet up where you will be able to meet people who are important to the anime community such as voice actor Victor Joseph Mignogna who is an American voice actor and musician known for his voice-over work in the English dubs of Japanese anime shows, the most notable being Edward Elric from the Fullmetal Alchemist series, for which he earned the American Anime Award for Best Actor in 2007.
